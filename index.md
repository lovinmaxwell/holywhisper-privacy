# Privacy Policy — HolyWhisper

**Last updated: March 9, 2026**

HolyWhisper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information**: Email address and display name when you sign up with Email/Password or Google Sign-In.
- **Prayer journal entries**: Text you write in the prayer journal, stored locally on your device.
- **Notes and highlights**: Bible notes, highlights, and bookmarks you create, stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Crash reports**: Anonymous crash and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included.
- **Authentication tokens**: Managed by Firebase Authentication. We do not store passwords.

---

## 2. Microphone / Voice Usage

HolyWhisper requests access to your device microphone **only** for the optional voice-to-text feature in the Prayer Journal, allowing you to dictate prayers by voice.

- Microphone audio is processed **on-device** by your operating system's speech recognition service.
- We do **not** record, transmit, or store any audio data on our servers.
- You can deny microphone permission — all other features work without it.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email / Google account | Authentication and account management |
| Prayer journal & notes | Stored locally on your device only |
| Crash reports | Improving app stability (anonymous) |
| Microphone audio | On-device speech-to-text only |

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage

- **Local data** (verses, highlights, notes, prayers, reading progress) is stored on your device using a local database. It does not leave your device unless you explicitly back it up.
- **Authentication data** is managed by Google Firebase, subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- Guest mode users have no data stored on our servers.

---

| Google Sign-In | Authentication | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

HolyWhisper is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 7. Data Retention & Deletion

- You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. This removes your authentication data from Firebase.
- Local app data (notes, prayers, highlights) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
