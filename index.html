Privacy Policy — HolyWhisper

Last updated: March 9, 2026

HolyWhisper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign up with Email/Password or Google Sign-In.
  • Prayer journal entries: Text you write in the prayer journal, stored locally on your device.
  • Notes and highlights: Bible notes, highlights, and bookmarks, stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Authentication tokens: Managed by Firebase Authentication. We do not store passwords.

2. Microphone / Voice Usage

HolyWhisper requests access to your device microphone only for the optional voice-to-text feature in the Prayer Journal.

  • Microphone audio is processed on-device by your operating system's speech recognition service.
  • We do not record, transmit, or store any audio data on our servers.
  • You can deny microphone permission — all other features work without it.

4. Data Storage

  • Local data (verses, highlights, notes, prayers, reading progress) is stored on your device. It does not leave your device.
  • Authentication data is managed by Google Firebase, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Guest mode users have no data stored on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

HolyWhisper is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen.
  • Local app data (notes, prayers, highlights) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
